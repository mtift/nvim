## Debugging with [xdebug](https://xdebug.org (sames shortcuts as [vdebug](https://github.com/vim-vdebug/vdebug))
- `<Space>db`: toggle breakpoint
- `<F5>`: start/continue (to next breakpoint/end of script)
- `<F2>`: step over
- `<F3>`: step into
- `<F4>`: step out
- `<F6>`: stop debugging
